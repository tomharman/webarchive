Overview

Any Dream Will Do was a prime time, Saturday night television show. School choirs around the UK were encouraged to upload a video of their best singing performance. Members of the public were able to enter an ‘audition room’ and vote on their favourites. The best acts were shortlisted before Andrew Lloyd Webber made the final decision on which choir got to perform live on the season finale.
